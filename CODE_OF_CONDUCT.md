# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want `core-lab` to be a professional, respectful, and welcoming engineering project.

Participants are expected to:
- communicate respectfully and constructively
- assume good intent while giving direct technical feedback
- focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior, not people
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or hostile conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be thoughtful in reviews and discussions.
- Provide actionable feedback.
- Welcome questions and different experience levels.
- Resolve disagreements with curiosity and professionalism.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation
- Harassment or discriminatory behavior
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and participation that violate this code when necessary to protect the project and its contributors.
